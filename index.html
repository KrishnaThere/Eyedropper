Eyedropper

This shall be a game where the player will have to select all the boxes which will have the color closest to the one shown on the side of the pixelated image.

With each increasing level, the plixelated grid will be denser and more resoluted. The image shall change after every 2 levels. After every 5 levels, the number of colors to select shall also increase.

Every level will have a time limit and the player will have to click a certain numbers of grid and pass a certain threshold number to move to the next level within the time limit
